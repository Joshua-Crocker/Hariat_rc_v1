Hariat,
Quick and easy kitchen and laundry services right at your finger tips.

What do we offer?

Hariat offers our customers with an application to make your kitchen and laundry service/installation needs effortless. In less than 7 clicks you can book a service request to your location, all you have to do is sit and wait for a technician to be dispatched to your location! Once a technician is on site, there is a built in chat feature where you can communicate to the technician through the app!

What makes our product so great?

As listed above, you can create a service request in less than seven clicks! Once you have an account created, within seven clicks you can book a service request or installation to your business without any hastle! Once a technician has been assigned you can communicate with them through the app, and even see their rating from other clients! And if you like your previous technician you can save them as a service provider and use them for your projects or service calls in the future!
